# In_A_Flash

Design Thinking Board:

B2B/BTC Marketing Agent
- Branding: Build logo, brand identify and builds a marketing plan from that
- Can create marketing plans for Enterprise or Startups
- Tracks performance from campaigns such as CTR/CAC and other KPIs
- Agent run ads campaigns in social, google,
- Social media posts: generate and review

Go to Market Strategy
- Focus on startups for Startups to reach their users
- example: AI Agent shows a GTM to Hacker

Video Idea:
- my enterprise name is this ...
- SOLO Founder - i don't have a marketing background
- can you build my brand from scratch and a marketing plan for the next 6 months
- which conferences can you do? which platforms can you use?
- video goes from solo founder putting together the plan to a hackathon where they will give out free tokens in exchange for potential lifetime subscriptions.

GenAI Video Script:

1. Shot 1 — The Founder sits at a clean desk, looking at product notes beside an open laptop showing a simple app interface as the opening voiceover begins. Camera: wide establishing shot, slow push-in. Duration: 1.8s. Mood/lighting: calm natural morning light, soft shadows, clean and professional.
2. Shot 2 — Close view of the laptop screen with product notes and a minimal software interface visible, reinforcing the unfinished launch planning stage. Camera: screen close-up, gentle static with slight handheld softness. Duration: 1.5s. Mood/lighting: neutral and focused, soft daylight reflections, readable screen.
3. Shot 3 — The Founder opens In A Flash and begins typing: “Create a brand plan, launch timeline, and content ideas for my software product.” Camera: over-the-shoulder medium close-up, subtle slide right. Duration: 2.0s. Mood/lighting: attentive and practical, bright but soft workspace tones.
4. Shot 4 — The typed prompt completes on screen as In A Flash begins organizing the response into clean sections. Camera: tight screen insert, slow push-in. Duration: 1.6s. Mood/lighting: modern and reassuring, crisp whites with blue and green accents.
5. Shot 5 — The interface clearly displays the section headings Audience, Message, Timeline, Content, and Next Steps in an orderly layout. Camera: straight-on screen macro shot, minimal tilt down. Duration: 1.7s. Mood/lighting: clear, structured, and calm, polished screen glow.
6. Shot 6 — The Founder uses the plan to update a website page, with quick visual progress across the laptop and notebook. Camera: medium side angle, smooth pan across desk. Duration: 1.6s. Mood/lighting: productive and steady, soft natural light with friendly workspace warmth.
7. Shot 7 — A fast sequence shows the Founder writing a short post, preparing a demo screen, and reviewing feedback notes while text on screen appears: Plan / Create / Share / Learn. Camera: mixed close-up inserts, quick flowing cuts with gentle motion. Duration: 2.0s. Mood/lighting: organized momentum, brighter light, still calm and minimal.
8. Shot 8 — The room feels brighter as the Founder leans back slightly, calm and focused, while the laptop shows organized notes, a calendar, and a checklist with completed items. Camera: medium hero shot, slow arc move. Duration: 1.8s. Mood/lighting: confident and peaceful, airy daylight, subtle sense of progress.
9. Shot 9 — End screen with clean branding: “In A Flash” and “Plan your launch.” Camera: static centered product end card, no movement. Duration: 1.0s. Mood/lighting: minimal, bright, polished, professional finish.
