# In_A_Flash

B2B/BTC Marketing Agent
- Branding: Build logo, brand identify and builds a marketing plan from that
- Can create marketing plans for Enterprise or Startups
- Tracks performance from campaigns such as CTR/CAC and other KPIs
- Agent run ads campaigns in social, google,
- Social media posts: generate and review

Go to Market Strategy
- Focus on startups for Startups to reach their users
- example: AI Agent shows a GTM to Hacker

Video Idea:
- my enterprise name is this ...
- SOLO Founder - i don't have a marketing background
- can you build my brand from scratch and a marketing plan for the next 6 months
- which conferences can you do? which platforms can you use?
- video goes from solo founder putting together the plan to a hackathon where they will give out free tokens in exchange for potential lifetime subscriptions.


Video Script
0:00 - 0:05 | The Hook
Visual: Close-up of a tired developer staring at a glowing screen in a dark room. Code reflections in his glasses.
Text on Screen: POV: You can code anything but have 0 marketing skills.
Voiceover (Internal Monologue): "I built a killer enterprise app. But my marketing budget is exactly zero dollars."
0:05 - 0:15 | The Problem & The Tool
Visual: The founder sighs, opens a browser tab, and goes to In A Flash.
Action: Fast-motion typing on the screen. We see the exact text entered: "enterprise name: SOLO Founder. No marketing background. Build my brand from scratch and a 6-month plan..."
Voiceover: "So I fed my raw idea into In A Flash."
0:15 - 0:30 | The Transformation (The Plan)
Visual: Rapid split-screen montage. On one side, In A Flash generates a 6-month roadmap, logos, and platform strategies. On the other side, the founder is frantically nodding, high-fiving the air, and executing.
Text on Screen:
Month 1: Build in Public 🚀
Month 3: X & LinkedIn Domination 📈
Month 5: The Ultimate Growth Hack 🛠️
Voiceover: "In seconds, it built a complete brand strategy. A 6-month blueprint. The exact platforms to target, like X and LinkedIn. And then, it gave me the ultimate growth hack."
0:30 - 0:45 | The Climax (The Hackathon)
Audio: [Beat drops heavy. Music gets high-energy.]
Visual: Cut to a bright, chaotic virtual hackathon dashboard. Discord chats flying by. A live counter ticks up: 1,200+ Developers Registered.
Text on Screen: FREE TOKENS FOR LIFETIME SUBS.
Voiceover: "Month 5: We launched a global hackathon. Giving away free utility tokens to builders in exchange for lifetime subscriptions."
0:45 - 0:55 | The Result
Visual: Back to the founder. He is smiling, relaxed, drinking coffee in daylight. The screen shows a Stripe or user dashboard exploding with new active users.
Voiceover: "We didn't just get users. We got an army of lifetime advocates. All built from a single prompt."
0:55 - 1:00 | Call to Action
Visual: Clean, bold closing screen with the In A Flash logo and website URL.
Text on Screen: Stop guessing. Launch In A Flash.
Voiceover: "Got tech but no marketing? Launch your startup In A Flash today."


Characters
The Relatable Innovator: Highly skilled in engineering and technology but completely blind when it comes to user acquisition.


Cinematic & Color Palette
The "Before" (0–15s): Dark mode aesthetic. Moody, cool tones (deep blues, cyans, charcoal greys). High contrast with the harsh, bright glow of a single monitor casting light on the founder's face.
The "After" (15–60s): Electric and vibrant. Pop neon colors (matrix green, electric purple, cyber pink). The lighting transitions to bright, warm daylight or high-energy RGB studio lighting to signal breakthrough and success.
📐 Camera Work & Framing
The Problem: Tight, claustrophobic close-ups. Macro shots of eyes reflecting code, fingers hovering over a mechanical keyboard, and stressed facial expressions. Static or slow, heavy camera movements. [1]
The Solution: Ultra-fast, dynamic whip-pans, snap-zooms, and jittery handheld camera movement. Point-of-view (POV) screen recordings should use smooth, simulated digital zooms to highlight text generation.
📝 Typography & Graphics
Font Choice: Clean, high-tech monospaced fonts (like JetBrains Mono, SF Mono, or Fira Code) to maintain the developer theme.
Text Animation: Kinetic typography that snaps into place with a subtle glitch or terminal-typing effect.
UI Elements: Clean, flat, modern SaaS dashboard design. Minimize visual clutter—only show big bold numbers, rapid text generation, and scaling chart lines. [1]
🎵 Audio & Sound Design
Music Track: A retro-modern Synthwave or Cyberpunk track. It starts with a slow, ambient, mysterious synth pad and builds anticipation until a massive, bass-heavy beat drop at the 30-second mark.
SFX (Sound Effects):
Heavy, mechanical keyboard clacks.
Digital "woosh" and data-processing sounds when In A Flash generates the plan.
Crowded stadium roar or rapid digital notification pings during the hackathon climax.
